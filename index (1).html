04 · Entrepreneurship

Is entrepreneurship driven by opportunity — or desperation?

In high-income economies (teal ▲), new businesses tend to form even when unemployment is low — opportunity-driven. In emerging economies (gold ●), the correlation is messier, suggesting necessity-driven entrepreneurship.

05 · Banking & Income

Do young people with bank accounts end up in richer countries?

Youth financial inclusion strongly predicts national income — but the direction of causality cuts both ways. Wealthier countries make banking easier; early banking habits may also build financial awareness that compounds over time.
